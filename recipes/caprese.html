Caprese

Italian master salad

Fresh salad that will make you remember the smells and the taste of the Mediterranean Sea.

Ingredients

  • 4 large ripe tomatoes
  • 1 pound fresh mozzarella
  • 1/3 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 3 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
  • salt and black pepper

Steps

  1. Cut Tomatoes
  2. Cut mozzarella
  3. Alternate and overlap tomato slices and mozzarella slices on a plate
  4. Add basil leaves
  5. Drizzle with olive oil
  6. Add salt and pepper
