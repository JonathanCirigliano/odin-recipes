Pizza Margherita

Queen of all pizzas

Invented by Raffaele Esposito in honor of Italian Queen Margherita di Savoia.

Ingredients

  • 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 11 cup water
  • 1 package active dry yeast
  • 1 pinch white sugar
  • 1/4 cup flour for dusting
  • 2 cups pizza sauce
  • 20 slices fresh mozzarella
  • 20 leaves fresh basil
  • olive oil
  • sea salt to taste

Steps

  1. Stir flour and 1 teaspoon salt in a bowl.
  2. Mix water, yeast, and sugar in a large bowl.
  3. Stir half the flour mixture into yeast mixture until no dry spots remain. Stir in remaining flour, 1/2 cup at a time, mixing well
  4. Lightly oil a large bowl, then place dough in the bowl and turn to coat with oil. Cover with a light cloth and let it rest in a warm place for 1 hour.
  5. Preheat oven with a pizza stone to 500 degrees F.
  6. Stretch out and pat 1 dough ball to form a circle 10 to 12 inches in diameter. Top with tomato and mozzarella.
  7. Slide the pizza in the oven. Bake until cheese is bubbly and the underside of the crust is golden brown.
